## Visual Specifications for the LinkedIn Graphic

**Brand Aesthetic & Philosophy:** SoapAI's visual identity for this post embraces a minimalist, clean, and forward-thinking aesthetic, reflecting trust and precision in healthcare technology. The design philosophy prioritizes clarity, ample negative space, and high-contrast readability, using subtle, organic elements to balance the technological aspect with human empathy. The overall feel is intelligent, precise, and approachable.

**Typography:**

| Font Role         | Canva Font Recommendation | Suggested Sizing / Weight     | Use Case                                  |
| :---------------- | :------------------------ | :---------------------------- | :---------------------------------------- |
| **Primary Header**| Montserrat                | Bold, 48-60pt                 | Main title, hero statement                |
| **Sub-Header**    | Montserrat                | Medium/Regular, 24-30pt       | Secondary information, supporting phrases |
| **Body Text**     | Lato                      | Regular, 14-16pt, line-height 1.4 | Explanatory text, lists, details          |

*Typography Readability Rule*: Ensure high contrast between text and background. Never place body text over busy or highly textured backgrounds. Maintain generous line spacing for readability.

**Custom Color Palette:**

| Color Role        | Color Name      | HEX Code  | Distribution (60-30-10 Rule) | Best Used For                          |
| :---------------- | :-------------- | :-------- | :--------------------------- | :------------------------------------- |
| Dominant (60%)    | Pristine White  | `#F9FAFC` | Backgrounds, primary structural elements | Core background space                 |
| Secondary (30%)   | Deep Teal       | `#0F4C5C` | Main text, secondary text, illustrations | Headers, subheaders, primary shapes    |
| Accent (10%)      | Fresh Mint      | `#A1E5C1` | Highlight boxes, CTAs, icons | Call outs, critical metrics, button highlights |
| Support           | Soft Stone Gray | `#8E9CA8` | Borders, subtle gradients    | Lines, subtle containers               |

**Layout & Composition:** The graphic will feature a clean, balanced composition. A strong focal point will be the hero headline, prominently displayed. Elements will be structured using a subtle grid, respecting the rule of thirds, with a bias towards central or left-aligned content. Generous negative space (at least 40% of the graphic) will enhance clarity and a premium feel. Small, abstract representations of AI or healthcare elements (e.g., neural networks, molecular structures, subtle data visualizations) will be subtly integrated without distracting from the main message.
